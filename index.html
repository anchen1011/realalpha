PriceList

PriceList is a coded and functional price list in a grid format.

The industry standard for unit pricing is in the Grid Format and List Format. When you partner with RealPrices, we will provide both formats, with prices optimized using our proprietary coding system. You could either specify an average rental or sales value per square foot, or you can let RealPrices recommend an average based off our market analysis and insight.

PriceOptim

PriceOptim is a rental and condo price optimization.

RealPrices allows real estate developers to optimize your top line revenues through a price optimization software that monitors the real time demand of your units. About Us

RealPrices is lead by a team of experts in real estate development and machine learning. On the real estate development side, we have cumulatively over 10 years of experience and various real estate degrees from MIT and McGill University. On the computer science side, we are led by an expert in machine learning and artificial intelligence who has started multiple successful data driven firms in China. Together, we are the ideal team to provide a data driven real estate product.

To date, RealPrices has priced over 1,500 condo and apartments, adding approximately 6% in rental value to existing stabilized rental projects and about 2.5% to apartment buildings during the launch phase.
